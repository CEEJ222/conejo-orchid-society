At Every Meeting

What to Expect at Monthly Meetings

Our monthly member meetings are the heart of COS — a welcoming space to learn, share, and connect with fellow orchid enthusiasts. Meetings are held the second Monday of every month at 7:00 PM at Westlake Village Town Hall. No meetings in August or December.

✦
Guest Speaker
Expert presentations on orchid culture, genera, and growing techniques.
✦
Q&A Session
Bring your questions — our experienced members and speakers are here to help.
✦
Culture Session
Hands-on growing advice covering light, water, fertilizer, and more.
✦
Plant Judging
Members bring their blooming plants for display and judging.
✦
Plant Raffle
Take home something beautiful — every meeting features a raffle of orchid plants.
✦
Refreshments
Light refreshments served at each meeting.
